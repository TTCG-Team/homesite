WHAT IS VOIDLIT?

Voidlit is an on chain trading card game (TCG) empowering collectors to truly own their cards and other game items. Voidlit supports Solana and TON and enables cross chain information exchange, and will support more blockchains in the future. The game is currently in the beta phase.

WHAT IS VOIDLIT COIN?

Voidlit Coin (VC) is a governance token for Voidlit. Players can use VC to purchase game card packs, participate in card making schemes, vote for banned card lists, and more.

WHEN WILL THE GAME BE RELEASED?

The game is currently in the beta testing phase. View our roadmap here.

WHAT DO YOU NEED TO PREPARE TO PARTICIPATE IN THE GAME?

You will need at least one complete deck consisting of 40 Voidlit cards. If you haven’t purchased Voidlit cards, the game provides a basic deck that allows you to participate in the game.

WHAT IS THE RARITY OF VOIDLIT CARDS?

Voidlit Cards are classified into N, R, SR, SSR, UR, and L cards based on their strength and rarity. Each version of UR and L cards will have a limited issuance quantity. Voidlit Card will continue to release new versions of Voidlit Cards. The new version of extended cards will not affect the rarity of previously released cards.

HOW TO OBTAIN A VOIDLIT CARD?

There are various ways to obtain Voidlit Cards. Most of cards can be obtained through card packs in card stores. Each card pack will contain 5 cards. A certain portion of UR cards and L cards will be auctioned through a hot auction.

WHAT ARE THE REWARDS AND PUNISHMENTS FOT WINNING OR LOSING IN GAME BATTLES?

The game mode is divided into ladder mode and regular battle. In ladder mode, after reaching a certain level, players can choose to activate sacrifice. If the player wins the battle, they will have the opportunity to randomly draw a card from the opponent’s hand. Conversely, if they lose the battle, they will be randomly drawn a card and handed over to the opponent. The higher the level of the card, the lower the probability of it being drawn.

At the end of each season, the system will reward users with Voidbit Coins, Voidbit Cards, or other game items based on the highest segment achieved.

DO I NEED A WEB3 WALLET TO PARTICIPATE IN THE GAME?

The game provides a basic deck table, and players can use it to battle against other players without connecting their wallets. But if players want to have a complete gaming experience, they need to connect to a Web3 wallet.

WHAT Web3 WALLET DO I NEED?

Voidlit currently supports Solana wallet and TON wallet.
